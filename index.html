Common questions

Things You're Probably Wondering

Straight answers, no agency-speak.

How much does a project cost? ▼
Custom websites: $2,000–$6,000. Operations software and mobile apps: $25,000–$60,000+ for V1. We define scope, timeline, and cost before writing any code - no surprises.
How long does it take to build? ▼
Custom websites: 1–4 weeks. Scoped V1 software: 6–12 weeks depending on complexity. The timeline holds because we define scope before we start.
Do I own the code when it's done? ▼
Yes, fully. Your custom system is yours upon final payment - no vendor lock-in, no subscription required to keep using it. We build on proven internal frameworks that speed up delivery, but everything built specifically for your business belongs to you.
What happens after launch? ▼
Every project ships with a bug warranty - 90 days for websites, up to 150 days for custom software. Beyond that, most clients move to a managed services retainer: dedicated hosting, direct access to the person who built the system, ongoing monitoring, and feature development at a reduced rate. It's like having a part-time tech person on call who already knows your codebase.
I just have an idea, not a spec. Is that okay? ▼
Absolutely. Most of our best projects started as a rough conversation. For software, that's what the discovery phase is for - we help you define exactly what's needed before building.
Do you set up Google Business and Search Console too? ▼
Yes. Many clients want the site plus GMB claimed and optimized, Search Console verified, analytics set up, and full account handoff so you own everything. That's often bundled with local and service-business sites.
