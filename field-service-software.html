What we build

Field Service Software Built for Edge Cases

Standard features matter, but the differentiator is handling the messy, high-friction workflows generic tools structurally can't model.

🗺️

Dispatch & Route Planning

Assign jobs to techs, see who's available, and build optimized daily routes. Dispatch updates push to the tech's phone instantly - no phone tag with the office.

📱

Mobile Check-Ins

Techs check in on arrival, complete job checklists, capture photos, and mark jobs done - all from their phone. No paper, no transcribing into a spreadsheet later.

🔄

Recurring Schedules

Set up weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly recurring jobs that auto-populate to the schedule. Manage seasonal adjustments, skips, and holds without rebuilding the schedule manually.

📋

Job Checklists & Notes

Custom checklists for each service type. Techs fill them out in the field. Managers see completions in real-time. Clients see a summary in their portal.

💳

Invoicing & Payments

Generate invoices from completed jobs. Collect payment via Stripe or integrate with QuickBooks. Automate recurring billing for maintenance contracts.

📈

Manager Dashboard

See all jobs, all techs, all routes on one live dashboard. Track completion rates, job times, revenue per route, and outstanding invoices - without digging through spreadsheets.

🧠

Intelligent Time Capture - The Assumption Engine

Field techs shouldn't have to build their timecard from scratch. The Assumption Engine - our passive time tracking system - builds a draft timecard automatically from GPS speed patterns and job assignments. Techs confirm in under a minute. End-of-week submissions become a tap-through, not a memory test.

Common questions

Field Service Software FAQ

Can field techs use this on their phone? ▼
Yes. We build mobile-first so your crew can see their route, log check-ins, capture photos, complete checklists, and mark jobs done - all from their phone, with or without reliable cell service (offline-capable builds available).
Does it integrate with QuickBooks for invoicing? ▼
Yes. We commonly build two-way QuickBooks Online and Xero integrations so completed jobs flow directly into invoicing without manual re-entry. Stripe payment collection is also a standard option.
How is this different from ServiceTitan or Jobber? ▼
Generic tools have fixed workflows and per-seat pricing. Custom software is built around your exact process, including the edge cases those platforms structurally can't handle - custom pay logic, no-address job sites, unusual dispatch flows, or time tracking that has to work in the real world. You also own the code outright - no vendor risk.
How long does it take to build? ▼
Most V1 field service systems ship in 6–12 weeks. The timeline is defined and fixed at the start based on what's in scope for V1. No surprises, no open-ended timeline.
Can it handle GPS or location tracking? ▼
Yes. We can build technician location tracking, job-site verification (check-in within a radius of the address), and live map views of where your crew is. Scope determines which level of location features makes sense for V1.
Do we own the source code? ▼
Always. All source code, databases, and infrastructure configurations are handed to you on final payment. You can hire any developer to maintain it. No subscription, no vendor dependency.
