Common questions

Website Design FAQ

Straight answers about custom web design in Utah.

How long does a website take to build? ▼
Most sites ship in 1–4 weeks. Simple 5-page sites can be live in 7–10 business days. Complex sites with 10+ pages and integrations are typically 3–4 weeks. The timeline is fixed at the start - no surprises.
Do you use WordPress, Squarespace, or Wix? ▼
No. We write custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This means faster load times, cleaner code, better security, and a site that doesn't look like a template. You're not locked into any platform or subscription.
What does the SEO setup actually include? ▼
Every site ships with: optimized title tags and meta descriptions, structured data (JSON-LD schema), XML sitemap, robots.txt, Google Business Profile setup & optimization, Google Search Console verification, and Google Analytics 4 configured. You own all of it.
Do I own the code when it's done? ▼
Yes - always. All source code is yours upon final payment. No monthly fees, no vendor lock-in, no platform subscriptions. You can host it anywhere and modify it with any developer.
Can you help with copywriting and content? ▼
Yes. We offer copywriting as an add-on for clients who need help writing page content. We can also help you structure your service descriptions and calls-to-action for better conversions.
Do you work with clients outside Salt Lake City? ▼
Absolutely. We're remote-first and work with clients across Utah - Provo, Ogden, St. George, and beyond - as well as nationwide. All design reviews and calls are done virtually.
What happens after launch? ▼
We hand off all accounts (Google Analytics, Search Console, GMB) so you own everything. Every website ships with a 90-day bug warranty. After that, most clients move to managed services for hosting, monitoring, and direct access for updates.
